### Q: E/DartVM (14216): WebSocketException: Invalid WebSocket upgrade request
### T: to enable flutter run normal
### A: 环境变量文件 ~/.bash_profile or ~/.zshrc 添加这句: `export NO_PROXY=localhost,127.0.0.1`
